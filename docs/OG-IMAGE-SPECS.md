# OG Image Specifications for Automatización 101

**Filename**: `/public/og-automation-101.png`

- **Width**: 1200px
- **Height**: 630px

- **Format**: PNG (for best quality) or JPG

## Design Specifications

### Background
- Black gradient background: `#050505` → `#0a0a0a` → `#0f0f0f`
- Neon gradient accent border/line (optional)

### Text Content
1. **Main Title** (Top/Center)
   - "AUTOMATIZACIÓN 101"
   - Font: Bold, sans-serif
   - Size: ~80-100px
   - Color: `#00ff88` (neon green)

2. **Subtitle** (Below title)
   - "Tu inicio garantizado: domina la lógica de la automatización aunque empieces desde cero"
   - Font: Medium weight
   - Size: ~32-40px
   - Color: White or light gray

3. **Key Benefits** (3 bullet points or badges)
   - "4 Sesiones en Vivo"
   - "100% Gratuito"
   - "Desde el 14 de Enero"
   - Style: Small badges with gradient background
   - Color: Gradient from `#00ff88` to `#00cfff`

4. **Branding** (Bottom right)
   - "idir.ai"
   - Small logo (if you have one)
   - Font: Bold
   - Color: `#00ff88`

### Visual Elements
- Neon glow effects around text
- Circuit board pattern (subtle, in background)
- Automation icons (gears, arrows, connections) - subtle
- Gradient line separator between sections

## Testing Checklist

After creating the image:

1. **File Location**: Verify it's at `/public/og-automation-101.png`
2. **Dimensions**: Confirm 1200x630px exactly
3. **File Size**: Keep under 1MB (ideally under 500KB)
4. **Preview**: Test with Facebook Debugger
5. **Mobile**: Check how it looks on mobile previews
6. **Text Readability**: Ensure text is readable when small
